Sanrio is pleased to announce that, together with Netflix, has decided to produce a live-action Netflix series “Gudetama: An Eggcellent Adventure” featuring the character Gudetama, a lazy and unmotivated egg character, and will distribute it exclusively worldwide by the end of 2022.

This Netflix series is a road-tripping tale where Gudetama goes in search of their mother. It showcases the main characters created using the latest computer graphic technology and is designed to resonate with all the unmotivated folks.
