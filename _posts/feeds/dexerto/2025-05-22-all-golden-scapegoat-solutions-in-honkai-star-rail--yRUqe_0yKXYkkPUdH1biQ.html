If you’re banging your head against the wall with the Golden Scapegoat puzzles in Honkai: Star Rail 3.3, you’re not alone.

These things are brutal. Between the shadow that copies your every move and the pressure to light all the altars just right, it’s easy to mess up and have to start over (again and again). We get it, it’s not just about figuring out the path, it’s about thinking five steps ahead without losing your mind.

Need help solving the puzzles? Here’s a step-by-step guide to find and solve them all.

Lightless Chapel Dawncloud Golden Scapegoats

Golden Scapegoat #1

Follow these steps to solve the puzzle: Right, right, right, left, right, up, left, right, left, right, then straight to the exit.

Rewards: Scholar’s Tweed Jacket, Golded Remains

Golden Scapegoat #2

Follow these steps to solve the puzzle: Left, down, right, left, down, right, left, up, down, up, down, left, left, and exit.

Rewards: Poet Dill Wreath, Golden Remains

Golden Scapegoat #3

Follow these steps to solve the puzzle: Left, down, right, right, right, left, left, left, left, up, right, right, and exit.

Rewards: Poet’s Gilded Bracelet, Golden Remains

Golden Scapegoat #4

Follow these steps to solve the puzzle:

Left, left, left, right, right, right, right, right, right. At this point, the shadow will start following you, so repeat your own steps until you fall. Then, head left until you’re two steps past the altar. Here, go upward and right until you’re right past the altar. The next steps are to go down and to the left.

Rewards: Poet’s Dill Wreath, Lightless Chapel Dawncloud Hymn Tablet.

Fallen Twilight City Okhema

Golden Scapegoat #1

Follow these steps to solve the puzzle: Right, right, right, right, left, left, left, left, right, right, right, up, down, up, and exit.

Rewards: Poet’s Star Studded Skirt, Golden Remains

Golden Scapegoat #2

Follow these steps to solve the puzzle: Left, right, down, right, right, left, right, left, left, left, right, right, left, then exit.

Rewards: Scholar’s Felt Snowboots, Golden Remains

Fortress of Dome Eye of Twilight

Golden Scapegoat #1

Follow these steps to solve the puzzle: Left, left, left, right, right, right, right, right, right, left, right, left, left, left, right, right, left, left.

Rewards: Scholar’s Auxiliary Knuckle, Golden Remains.

Golden Scapegoat #2

Follow these steps to solve the puzzle: Right, right, right, right, left, left, up, left, left, right, left, right, left, right.

Rewards: Poet’s Dill Wreath, Golden Remains

Golden Scapegoat #3

Follow these steps to solve the puzzle: Left, left, right, right, right, up, left, left, left, right. At this point, your shadow will start moving, so go right four times, left three times, then right again, and then left until the exit.

Rewards: Poet’s Gilded Bracelet, Golden Remains

Golden Scapegoat #4

Follow these steps to solve the puzzle: Left, left, right, right, right, right, right. Then, go left, up, left, right, down, left, right, up, and right until you reach the exit.

Rewards: Poet’s Gilded Bracelet, Golden Remains

Cloudedge Bastion Ruins Eye of Twilight

Golden Scapegoat #1

Follow these steps to solve the puzzle: Left, left, right, right, right, left, left. Then, go left, right, right, right, left, left, and exit.

Rewards: Scholar’s Tweed Jacket, Golden Remains

Golden Scapegoat #2

Follow these steps to solve the puzzle: Left, left, right, right, right, right, right. Then, left, left, right, right, left, left, left.

Rewards: Scholar’s Tweed Jacket, Golden Remains

Golden Scapegoat #3

Follow these steps to solve the puzzle: Left, left, left, right, up, left. Then, right, up, right, right, right, right, left, right.

Rewards: Scholar’s Tweed Jacket, Golden Remains
