Call of Duty is leaning into its stylish side with the new Ballerina event, a limited-time crossover inspired by the upcoming Ballerina movie starring Ana de Armas.

This event brings a new assassin-chic aesthetic to Black Ops 6 and Warzone, complete with elegant-but-lethal Operator skins, weapon blueprints, and a deadly new rifle.

Here’s everything you need to know about the Ballerina event, including dates, how it works, and a full breakdown of what’s up for grabs

The Ballerina event runs from June 5, 2025, until June 12, 2025. It’s a one-week crossover that ties directly into the release of the Ballerina film, and it’s live across Multiplayer, Zombies, and Warzone.

Event explained

The Ballerina event is a themed reward track that lets you earn cosmetics and weapons by simply playing the game.

There are no complicated objectives, just rack up XP and hit event milestones. All standard rewards are unlocked through gameplay, while one bonus reward is exclusive to BlackCell Battle Pass owners.

Expect 10 total rewards across all modes, plus an 11th unlockable item if you’ve bought into the BlackCell premium track. This is where you’ll score the Essex Model 07, a brand-new marksman rifle, and a ballerina-themed nunchuck melee weapon blueprint.

All Rewards

Tracer Pack: Ballerina includes:

  • Two Eve Operator Skins: red sequin dress (Eve Macarro) and black tactical (Ruska Rogue)
  • Three Weapon Blueprints with High Table Coin Tracers and Death FX: Relevé (AK-74 Assault Rifle), Plié (Saug SMG), and Arabesque (9mm PM Pistol).
  • Dance With Death Finishing Move
  • Pirouette Emote
  • Keepsake Weapon Charm
  • Caged Grace Animated Emblem
  • Pas de Deux Animated Calling Card
  • Take a Bow Loading Screen

Pricing hasn’t been confirmed, but bundles like this usually land around 2,400 COD Points, or $20–$25.
