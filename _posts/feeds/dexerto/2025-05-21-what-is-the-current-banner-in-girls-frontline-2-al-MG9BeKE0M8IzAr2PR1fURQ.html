Banners in Girls’ Frontline 2: Exilium let players unlock new Elite Dolls and add them to their rosters. So, here are all the current character banners and weapon banners you can roll on. 

The current Girls’ Frontline 2 banner rotation allows players to obtain new units and try out different team comps. Like most Gacha games, these rotate regularly and can drastically change the meta. 

If you’re looking to spend your hard-earned Targeted Access Permissions and standard Access Permissions on the latest and upcoming characters, we’ve got all the details below.

These are all the banners you can currently pull on in Girls Frontline 2: 

  • Beginner banner
  • Standard banner
  • Klukai banner
  • Makiatto rerun banner
  • Weapon banner

When do the banners end?

The current GFL2 banners will run from May 1 to May 21. However, the standard banner will be permanently available and won’t expire on the date posted above. 

Beginner banner 

As the name suggests, the beginner banner is only available to new players. Every Girls’ Frontline 2 account gets 50 pulls on this banner, and after you have used up your 50 pulls, the beginner banner will no longer be available.

Unlike pulls on the game’s limited character and limited weapon banners, the beginner banner has a 20% discount and requires only eight Standard Permissions to perform a 10 pull. 

If that wasn’t exciting enough, you’re guaranteed to get an Elite Doll before or when you hit the 50-pull cap, which makes this a must for every player. 

Standard banner 

Just like in Genshin Impact and Honkai Star Rail, Girls’ Frontline 2’s standard banner is permanent and will always remain in rotation. It’s important to note that all characters aside from the Elite Dolls featured in the limited banners can be pulled here. 

Fortunately, Elite Dolls and Signature Weapons can be selected after you’ve performed 260 standard banner pulls. All the SSR characters and weapons can be found below:

Characters

  • Qiongjiu
  • Tololo
  • Sabrina
  • Vepley
  • Peritya
  • Mosin-Nagant

Weapons

  • Golden Melody: Assault Rifle
  • Optical Illusion: LMG
  • Mezzaluna: Shotgun
  • Heart Seeker: Shotgun
  • Planeta: Assault Rifle
  • Samosek: Sniper Rifle

When it comes to choosing the SSR character, we recommend selecting either Qiongjiu or Tololo. These two characters are among the best DPS units in Girls’ Frontline 2 – in fact, they appear within the top spots of our GFL2 tier list. 

It’s important to note that you’ll be guaranteed to pull a Standard Doll, Standard Weapon, Elite Doll, or Signature Weapon every 10 pulls. However, Elite Dolls and Signature Weapons have a 0.6% pull rate. 

This rate goes up to 1.89% after you’ve made 58 pulls without securing an Elite Doll or weapon. So, bear the above statistics in mind when you’re trying to build Pity on the Standard banner. 

Yoohee banner (limited character)

Yoohee is an SSR Support unit that wields an SMG. Her abilities are tailored around delivering Physical attacks, buffing her allies, and lowering enemy units’ defenses.

She’s particularly potent when paired with Daiyan and Ullrid, as whenever these units deal Physical damage, Yoohee gains additional Confectance Index points.

This makes Yoohee a must-have unit for any players who want to run Physical damage teams, so it’s well worth pulling for her while her banner is available. Yoohee’s banner runs from May 22 to June 11.

The following Dolls are also on this banner:

  • Littara (Sentinel)
  • Ksenia (Support)

Centaureissi banner rerun (limited character)

The second banner features Centaureissi. She’s a great SSR Support unit that deals Burn damage to enemies. While not essential, Centaureissi is a great replacement for Ksenia in Burn teams. In fact, when paired with QJ, Sharkry, and Vector, you’ll have a great mix of DPS and supportive heals. Centaureissi’s banner runs from May 22 to June 11.

You can also get the following Dolls on this banner:

  • Groza (Bulwark)
  • Colphne (Support)

Weapon banners

Like the limited character banners, the limited weapon banner features the latest SSR weapon with a boosted drop rate. From May 22 to June 11, players can unlock Yoohee’s signature weapon — Sparkling Centerstage.

It’s important to note that the pull rate for obtaining a Signature weapon on the Limited weapon banner is 0.7%, but you’ll be guaranteed a Signature Weapon at 70 pulls.

It’s important to note that players will need to have completed the Story Campaign Normal Stage [SL-1-2] before they can access this banner.

Next GFL2 banner

On April 25, Sunborn confirmed that both Yoohee and Zhaohui would follow after Vector’s banner. With Yoohee releasing on May 22, it’s safe to say that Zhaohui will follow sometime after June 11.
