The Switch 2 will launch with free and paid upgrades that enhance the visuals and performance of several Switch games, like Pokemon Scarlet & Violet, Zelda: Echoes of Wisdom, Super Mario Odyssey, and more.

While the Switch 2 editions of games like Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom and Hogwarts Legacy will require players to purchase an upgrade pack, Nintendo has now revealed details about all the free Switch game enhancements that will be released at launch.

The Switch 2 upgrades may improve graphics, performance, and even add support for features such as GameShare to existing Switch games. Each title offers different improvements, so to make your life easier, we’ve listed every free Switch 2 game upgrade and all the enhancements they will receive.

All free Switch 2 game upgrades

There are currently 12 Nintendo Switch games that will be getting free enhancement updates on the Switch 2. The free enhancements will be available to download alongside the Switch 2 launch on June 5, 2025.

It’s important to note that Nintendo’s GameChat feature will be free to all Switch 2 players until March 31, 2026. Afterwards, a Nintendo Switch Online membership will be required.

All paid Switch 2 game upgrades

There are currently six paid Switch 2 upgrade games. It’s important to note that if you already own the Switch version of the titles below, you can upgrade to the Switch 2 Edition for a cheaper price.

We’ll update this list as soon as Nintendo announces more Switch 2 game upgrades, so be sure to bookmark this page and check back regularly.
