A Valorant Mobile leaker has revealed the game will be receiving a plethora of social features, with one of them reportedly working similarly to TikTok.

Leaks regarding Valorant Mobile have been circulating around the internet for a while, but it was only on April 21, 2025, that we gotten an official confirmation from Riot the game is actually real and coming soon. Naturally, there was a lot of hype around the official announcement, considering the idea of playing the game on the go is an enticing one.

Regardless of fans’ excitement, though, there’s a huge catch. Not much is known about the game’s global release date at the time of writing, as Riot is only holding playtests exclusively in China to begin with.

That said, almost a month after this announcement, new details regarding the game have already emerged. Based on leaked information, it appears Valorant Mobile could introduce a new feature that’s similar to TikTok.

All leaked features for Valorant Mobile

According to a leaker in a post on X, the game will have features not available in the PC version—and if there’s one that stands out more than the rest, it has to be the “TikTok-style feature.” 

The way this feature works is described as a “TikTok-like shorts system”, allowing players to be able to post clips and like others’ clips. Other social features could also be present, such as the Global Chat system, the gifting system, and the In-game recruitment. 

These have yet to be elaborated on, but it’ll be interesting to see how exactly they’ll be implemented later.

Additionally, though it’s still in the early days, it’s safe to assume these are likely possible. Mobile Legends: Bang Bang, a well-known title on the same platform, has already done something similar, albeit with watching other players’ live streams directly in the game.

Aside from these social features, other ones that have leaked and are worth mentioning are the replay system, Spectator Mode (VCT format), custom rooms, highlight clips, and custom load-outs for each agent’s cosmetics, among other additions.

Meanwhile, cosmetics-wise, it’s suggested that there’ll be free skins that can be obtained from missions. Either way, the title is already shaping up to be quite an exciting one so far.
