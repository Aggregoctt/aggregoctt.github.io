Twitch star Tyler ‘Ninja’ Blevins clapped back at a viewer who asked him why he avoids Fortnite’s popular build mode. 

Over the last few years, Fortnite has undergone some pretty big changes. Building was the one big feature that used to set it apart from the rest, however, Epic Games have added Zero Build. 

That new mode, coupled with the OG map, gave the battle royale a new lease of life. Thousands of players returned to give it another go, and they’ve stuck around for quite some time. 

That includes Ninja, who made it big thanks to the original Fortnite boom. He had swapped games during the lull, but returned with Zero Build and has stuck around pretty much ever since. However, some fans want him to go back to his roots and start cranking out 90s in the build mode again. 

Ninja can’t believe fan request to play Fortnite build mode

The request for him to go back to the popular build mode came up during his May 11 stream as Blevins clapped back at a viewer’s question about it. 

“Imagine being subbed to the channel, your first message, and I don’t know how he got gifted a sub but he said ‘why are you playing Zero Build you bot?’ I’m a f**king 33-year-old man, married, seven years,” Ninja said. 

“Do you understand that? I’m almost halfway to 40 years old and you’re asking me why I’m not playing build mode? Do you know how stupid that sounds? I want you to look in the mirror and think about that question the next time you open your mouth or type something in somebody’s chat. Really reflect on what you’re asking, ok?”

The Twitch star didn’t finish there, asking the viewer why would he choose to “sweat” against “15 and eight-year-olds” when he’s nearly 40. 

So, if you tune in, just expect to see Zero Build if he is playing Fortnite.
