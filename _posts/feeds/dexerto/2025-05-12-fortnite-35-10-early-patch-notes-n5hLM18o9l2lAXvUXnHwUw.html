Fortnite’s 35.10 update is set to introduce new weapons as well as other exciting additions in Chapter 6, Season 3.

Fortnite’s Chapter 6, Season 3 may not be long compared to the previous ones, but it’s packed with a plethora of new content to keep players busy. In this current Galactic Battle season, the game is confirmed to receive six major updates.

With a season dedicated to Star Wars, we’ve seen the entire loot pool be replaced with blasters, as well as old skins from the franchise returning to the shop. That’s not all there is to it, though.

The upcoming 35.10 update is shaping up to be huge, as fan-favorite characters will finally make their debut along with new weapons. Below, we’ve gathered everything you need to know that will be coming in the next patch.

The v35.10 update, also referred to as “Chew on This,” is set to release on May 15, 2025. This has been confirmed by Epic via their blog post.

Though the exact time isn’t confirmed yet, looking at previous updates, it will likely drop at 1:00 AM PDT / 4:00 AM EDT / 9:00 AM GMT.

Players can expect matchmaking to end shortly for maintenance before the update goes live and the servers are online. This usually lasts a few hours, but on rare occasions, it can take longer.

Wookie Bowcaster & CA-87 Jawa Scatter Blaster weapons

This update will bring two weapons to the loot pool, the first one being the returning Wookie Bowcaster, which was first added in Chapter 5, Season 2. This is a projectile weapon that deals a lot of damage the longer you charge it. Though it can also be fired normally.

On the other hand, the second weapon will be the all-new CA-87 Jawa Scatter Blaster. Not much is known about this weapon just yet, but assuming it works the same way in the franchise, it’s likely that it’s a deadly weapon in close-quarter fights.

General Grievous Skin quests 

During this update, the “Grevious Rewards” tab of the Battle Pass will finally be unlocked. As usual, expect to see new quests added as a way to earn rewards from this tab, which includes the General Grievous skin and additional cosmetics.

Aside from that, it’s likely that the super styles for this season will also be revealed and unlocked once this update drops as well.

Possible Death Jar Jar skin

According to leaked information, this update may also introduce the Death Jar Jar skin that was previously featured in the season’s cinematics. Just like in the trailer, this skin has him wearing a long black hoodie and cape.

That’s everything we know so far about the 35.10 update. On top of all these, there’s also a chance that there’ll be some map changes and we’ll keep you updated once more information emerges.
