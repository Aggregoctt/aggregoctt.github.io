Blizzard Entertainment

Diablo 4‘s demons are still angry, so your builds still need tweaking, and the bug list was long enough to warrant its own boss fight.

This Season 8 patch delivers some buffs and fixes several bugs. We’ve got QoL fixes, more poison, more power, and fewer ways to cheese your way past mechanics.

Needless to say, this patch is a big one. Here’s everything you need to know.

Spiritborn buffs

The Protector

  • Damage increased: 80% → 140%
  • Barrier amount: 15% → 30% Max Life
  • Thorns damage (Rank 5): 100% → 150%

Passives

  • Adaptive Stances: Core Stat bonus 50% → 75%
  • Vital Strikes: Vulnerable damage 45% → 80%; Healing 2% → 5% Max Life; Vigor gain doubled
  • Prodigy’s Tempo: Skill Rank bonus 2 → 4

Season 8: Belial’s Return

  • Oath Scrolls now purchasable from Sayeena for Spectral Ash
  • More consistent Betraying Eyes, better boss power clarity, easier-to-see Incursion icons
  • Sinerat, Hatred’s Embrace, Flesh Reaper all had their boss powers rebalanced

Barbarian

  • Ghor’s Devastating Grips: Now scales with DPS instead of ticks, roughly 100% more damage
  • Explosion Cooldown on Crit: 0.2 → 0.4
  • Twin Strikes: Damage 70–100% → 110–140%
  • Aspect of Dire Whirlwind: Now also boosts Crit Strike Damage while spinning

Druid

  • Earthen Might: Crit Damage 50% → 70%
  • Perfect Storm: Damage 60% → 80%
  • Humanity: Now also reduces damage by 2% per rank in Human Form
  • Gathlen’s Birthright: Lower Crit Hit requirements, faster Human form zoomies

Necromancer

  • Skeletal Priest: Buffed from 30% → 50% damage
  • Aspect of Reanimation: 25–45% → 40–60%
  • Sanguivor, Blade of Zir: Summon Damage from 60% → 400% (not a typo)

Rogue

  • Poison Trap: Damage 540% → 612%
  • Band of Ichorous Rose: Now up to 150% boost
  • Aspect of Contamination: Burst now deals extra % damage and buff lasts 9 seconds

Sorcerer

  • Shocking Impact: Now triggers on Stun refresh
  • Hail of Verglas: Damage per hit 1.5% → 2%, max now 100%
  • Glacial Aspect: No longer needs Blizzard cast and works with enchantment too

Bug Fixes

Vessel of Hatred

  • Spiritborn loot now drops properly
  • Fixed door-violating monsters in Vile Hive
  • Subo’s Seeker doesn’t tag scenery anymore
  • Gorshak DoT now dies with Gorshak
  • Hold Your Ground won’t break between waves

Base Game

  • Belial’s beams, Duriel’s burrow phase, Varshan’s attacks were aLL updated to behave like actual game mechanics
  • Pets won’t run away forever
  • Bone Spirits, Leap, and Poison Traps

UI/Accessibility

  • Screen Reader: more verbose, smarter, and less awkward
  • Materials tab actually updates
  • Compass no longer growing out of your character’s head

Cleanup & Misc Fixes

  • Crown of Lucion is fixed and re-enabled. No more infinite damage abuse.
  • Fixed several progression blockers in Season Journey, quests, and boss arenas
  • Boss Powers tuned, and now they can’t activate while mounted
  • Way fewer ways to break dungeons or glitch through encounters
