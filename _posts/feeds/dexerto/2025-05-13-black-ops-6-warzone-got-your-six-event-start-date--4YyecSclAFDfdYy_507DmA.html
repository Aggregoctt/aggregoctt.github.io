The latest Call of Duty Endowment event, Got Your Six, is coming to Black Ops 6 and Warzone, bringing plenty of free rewards for players to unlock.

Season 3 has been packed with events to get stuck into, most of which have been stoner-themed like Blaze of Glory and High Art. Now, with Season 4 on the horizon, the game is sobering up with the return of Call of Duty Endowment.

Got Your Six is set to be packed full of rewards, and a now-deleted trailer revealed that there’s a new Blueprint up for grabs. So, here’s everything you need to know about the event.

Got Your Six starts on Thursday, May 15, 2025, and will go live at 10 AM PT / 1 PM ET / 6 PM BST. It will run for two weeks and end on May 29, the same date that the Black Ops 6 and Warzone Season 4 update is scheduled to arrive.

How does it work?

It hasn’t been confirmed how Got Your Six will work just yet, but Call of Duty events usually let players earn rewards through one of a few different methods.

The first is by simply playing matches across any mode to earn XP, with rewards being handed out at specific milestones. Sometimes, there are also items you need to pick up from defeated enemies, which can then be spent on the prizes, like the Blaze Bucks from Blaze of Glory.

There have also been events that come with specific challenges that must be completed for each reward, forcing players to adjust their play style if they want to unlock them all. In these cases, there are three different sets of objectives for BO6 multiplayer, Zombies, and Warzone.

Given that we haven’t had one for a while, and the trailer specifically mentions the “Got Your Six Challenge,” we expect it to be the latter. However, it’s also possible that they have something completely new planned, so we’ll update this page as soon as we know more.

Rewards

A now-deleted trailer on the CoD Endowment YouTube channel accidentally revealed the eight rewards that players will be working towards. Check them out below:

  • Reinforced (Weapon Sticker)
  • Team Rally (Emblem)
  • One Hour Double Weapon XP Token
  • Covered Sixes (Calling Card)
  • Embattled (Spray)
  • Plated Pride (Weapon Charm)
  • One Hour Double XP Token
  • All Chipped Out (Ames 85 Blueprint)
