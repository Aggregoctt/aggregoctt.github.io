The Zenless Zone Zero 2.0 livestream has finally given players a look at Yixuan, Ju Fufu, and the new Waifei Peninsula. So, here’s everything you need to know about the upcoming banners and all the free anniversary rewards you can get. 

ZZZ’s 2.0 update is one of the most expansive yet, bringing with it the Waifei Peninsula district and two new S-rank characters. While both Yixuan and Ju Fufu may take the spotlight, there’s also the release of Pan Yinhu, a new A-rank Physical Defense unit who aims to give Kung Fu Panda a run for his money. 

On top of this, there’s a free S-rank character up for grabs and thousands of Polychromes to unlock as part of the anniversary event. So, to help get you up to speed, our ZZZ 2.0 update guide has everything you need all in one place. 

Zenless Zone Zero’s Version 2.0 “Where Clouds Embrace the Dawn,” will launch on June 6, which marks the beginning of Season 2.

Banners 

The following characters will be released during ZZZ 2.0 banner schedule:

Phase 1

  • Yixuan (S-rank, Auric Ink, Rupture)
  • Astra Yao (S-rank, Ether, Support)
  • Pan Yinhu (A-rank, Physical, Defense)

Phase 2

  • Ju Fufu (S-rank, Fire, Stun)
  • Caesar King (S-rank, Physical, Defense)

During Phase 1 of ZZZ’s 2.0 banner, players will be able to unlock Yixuan, the deadly S-rank Agent. Yixuan is known for her unique Attribute Auric Ink and Specialty Rupture, which ignores enemies’ Defense and deals damage based on her Sheer Force, which can be increased according to her Attack and Max HP. 

She also utilizes Adrenaline instead of regular Energy, and possesses two Ultimate modes. Her abilities will undoubtedly come in handy when it comes to dealing with the enemies in the Lemnian Hollow, which have tanky shields and enhanced abilities. 

Astra Yao will also be getting a rerun in Phase 1, while the new A-rank Pan Yinhu will be making his debut. Pan Yinhu is a Physical, Defense character who uses his paws to pummel his enemies into submission. 

Lastly, Phase 2 will see the release of S-rank Ju Fufu, the senior disciple of Yunkui Summit. Juu Fufu uses her weapon to accumulate Might and Momentum points, which deal Physical and Fire damage. 

Her Chain Attack grants the entire team the Tiger’s Roar effect, which boosts allies’ CRIT DMG as well as the damage of Chain Attacks and Ultimates. Phase 2 will also feature a Caesar King rerun, so if you have yet to unlock the Physical Defense unit, then now’s your chance. 

New location: Waifei Peninsula

During the 2.0 update, players will be able to explore Waifei Peninsula, an expansive district of New Eridu that is known for its vibrancy. The official press release notes that the region’s main city, Failume Heights, is renowned for its distinctive tea houses, pawnshops, and dessert stalls. 

If this new location wasn’t exciting enough, players will also be able to manage their very own Suibian Temple. It’s here where you’ll be able to upgrade the temple’s facilities and earn in-game rewards by dispatching Bangboos, completing commissions, and collecting resources from the Hollows.

Anniversary rewards 

To commemorate the game’s first anniversary, Version 2.0 will bring a wide array of celebration rewards, including a free S-Rank Agent, a free S-Rank W-Engine, 1,600 Polychromes, and 20 Encrypted Master Tapes.

You can see the full list and their quantities below : 

  • Standard S-rank W-Engine (Select 1 of 6)
  • x1,600 Polychromes 
  • x20 Encrypted Master Tapes 
  • x10 Boopon
  • x2 First Purchase Double Bonus Reset 
  • Belle & Wise outfit

Events 

There are a lot of events in ZZZ’s 2.0 update, so we’ve highlighted the major ones below for you:

Gravitational Attraction 

During the Gravitational Attraction, players can head over to the cinema in Lumina Square and enjoy movies with different characters. You’ll be able to obtain rewards by completing certain tasks, including an A-Rank W-Engine Reel Projector. 

Sould of Steel: Golden Bond

Soul of Steel: Golden Bond battle event sees players pilot mechs to duke it out against enemies in the Hollows. If you’re successful, you’ll earn rewards such as a namecard and Polychromes. 

Holographic Onslaught Arena

During this event, Proxies will take on daily combat challenges. You’ll only be able to team up with specific characters to face different bosses. 

Failume Heights Sightseeing Guide

Players can grab rewards by completing missions around Failume Heights – the game’s new city. 

New outfit: Yixuan Trails of Ink

Besides the new unlockable outfits for Belle and Wise, players will be able to purchase Yixuan’s Trails of Ink skin. Trails of Ink will be available from the in-game store and will be on sale for a limited time during the 2.0 event. After the event has ended, it will be purchaseable at its non-discounted price.
