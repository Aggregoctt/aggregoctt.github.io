YouTube and TikTok star Eugenia Cooney left fans worried after she began to gag during a live stream and abruptly ended the broadcast.

Eugenia Cooney is a prominent influencer across multiple platforms who specializes in fashion and beauty content.

Fans have regularly expressed concerns for Cooney’s health over the years, with the star suffering from an apparent eating disorder. In 2024, TikTok even blocked users from searching for her content, and instead directed them to the National Alliance for Eating Disorders.

In May 2025, Cooney, who has over 4M followers on TikTok and YouTube combined, once again sparked concerns from fans after suddenly ending her TikTok live broadcast when she claimed to have fallen ill.

Eugenia Cooney hasn’t been seen since concerning TikTok stream

During the broadcast, 30-year-old Cooney suddenly appeared to collapse and began to start gagging. The influencer then told her viewers that she “wasn’t feeling that great.”

“I think I might just get off,” she added. “I’m kinda letting my stress get to me. I’m gonna get off for now.”

In the time since, two videos have been posted to her TikTok channel, but, as reported by The Daily Mail, both were pre-recorded – with one supposedly being filmed a year ago.

Fans were very worried about her wellbeing following the livestream incident. “She genuinely looked so scared before she ended that live,” one commented.

“We all just want to see you get better baby,” another remarked on a pre-recorded video.

“That stare she was doing when she sat back was so scary and upsetting. I think this is the worst I’ve seen her,” another chimed in.

Eugenia’s friend, fellow influencer Jeffree Star, told viewers that Cooney was “clearly not ok” and fans have “no idea what she’s going through.”

At the time of posting Eugenia hasn’t responded to fans’ concerns on any platform.
