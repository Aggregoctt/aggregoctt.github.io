A League of Legends leaker has revealed a new “Spa Day” skinline, which will include a particular skin many dub as a fanservice.

Having been released all the way back in 2009, League of Legends is today known for its wide roster of champs as well as a plethora of cosmetic lines. From the iconic K/DA, Star Guardian, Dark Cosmic, and Spirit Blossom skins, among many others, anticipating the next set of skins is something many in the community often enjoy.

On May 21, 2025, new leaks circulated all over social media about “Spa Day” skins inspired from Eastern bathhouses, which will include Ahri.

Although it’s expected that the popular champ would also be included in the new line, considering how she’s been treated cosmetic-wise, the way this skin was described has caught many players off guard.

League of Legends’ Spa Day towel-only Ahri skin leaked

According to the leaker, this Ahri skin will be a Legendary one. Now, you may be thinking about a design that has plenty of little details. Well, as it turns out, it’s quite the opposite. The leaker said this skin features Ahri wearing only a towel.

Even YouTuber BigBadBear, the source of the leak, clarified this further in his video by writing: “Ahri is wearing a towel. Just a towel. No, I’m not kidding.”

Though this is still a leak and nothing is, of course, confirmed, fans couldn’t help but share their thoughts on social media. Some of them even went as far as poking fun at Riot’s financial situation.

“Yeah, they’re going broke lmfao,” commented one user.

“Financial situation seems a lot worse than we thought guys, I repeat it seems to be a lot worse,” said another.

That said, there were also others who celebrated after this information was shared.

One user wrote: “Thank the heavens some fan service is in route. After what happened to Leblanc’s cleavage, I lost all hope of the game being fun again.  All brutal joking aside, I’m curious to see what we actually get.
