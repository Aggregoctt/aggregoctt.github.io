When CBS first announced a Young Sheldon spinoff, its title quickly drew criticism. While we know Georgie ends up single in The Big Bang Theory, does he actually ever divorce Mandy?

As far as titles go, Georgie & Mandy’s First Marriage is a pretty wordy one. However, it’s also intriguing, with it clearly alluding to more than one round of nuptials. Adult Georgie is a single bachelor, with a string of ex-wives and girlfriends behind him when we first meet him in TBBT.

However, it’s never too clear who these women actually are. Sheldon is congratulated by two different ex-wives when he wins his Nobel prize, but as far as we know, Mandy is the only woman Georgie has wed.

Of course, there’s still a major gap between the two stories, but what if Georgie and Mandy married more than once? The theory has been floated before, and now one of the spinoff’s stars has weighed in on what might be further down the line.

Georgie and Mandy could have as many as three marriages by Big Bang Theory

Speaking to Dexerto, star Montana Jordan said fans could eventually see as many as three marriages if the Georgie & Mandy spinoff continues beyond its confirmed second season.

“I think that’s what’s great about it is this [question] is what keeps the audience on their toes,” he explained. “They get in these bickering fights and disagreements and all this and all that, but you’ve got to push through, that’s the reality of it. When you’re a young couple and have just had a newborn, there are some things that you have to push through. That’s the real life of it.

“But like you’re saying, the title is Georgie and Mandy’s First Marriage. Whether there’s a second or third marriage, there ain’t no telling. But I’ll be there regardless.”

With Georgie and Mandy so popular as a couple, many fans have hoped that they are endgame, making this first marriage theory more likely. However, this isn’t just a conclusion that viewers want, but also Mandy star Emily Osment herself.

“I’m hoping that I’m the ex-wife and the new wife,” Emily Osment explained when she was asked about the storyline by TV Line. “People get divorced and then they get remarried. Why can’t she [Mandy] be both?”

Another popular fan theory is that Georgie’s high-school crush Veronica comes back on the scene, and eventually becomes his second wife.

Veronica wasn’t seen in Young Sheldon after Season 3, where it was quietly hinted that she spent the rest of her life dedicated to charity work. After finding God in Season 2, Georgie also tries to devote himself to faith in order to date her, which doesn’t work out.

Season 3 finds Georgie and Veronica developing a friendship, eventually asking Veronica’s permission to ask Jana Owens out, staying with her into Season 4.

What actually lies in store for Georgie’s love life remains to be seen, but don’t expect him to be divorcing Mandy during Season 2.
