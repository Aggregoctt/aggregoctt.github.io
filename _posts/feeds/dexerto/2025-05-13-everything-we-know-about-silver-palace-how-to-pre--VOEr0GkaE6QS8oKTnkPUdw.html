Silver Palace is an upcoming fantasy world ARPG that combines flashy Genshin Impact-style combat with Victorian-inspired aesthetics. So, here’s everything you need to know, including how to pre-register and a sneak peek at the gameplay.

Silver Palace, otherwise known as “Victorian” Genshin Impact, has quickly caught the gacha game community’s attention. Not only do the steampunk-style visuals help differentiate from other free-to-play mobile games, but its sleek combat and unique character designs aim to rival industry giants like HSR, ZZZ, and WuWa.

On May 13, 2025, Silver Palace devs showcased a brand new trailer, showcasing over 10 minutes of gameplay. Not only did we get a good look at the two main protagonists, but we now have details on the Silver Palace’s story, setting, and how you can pre-register.

No, Silver Studio has yet to reveal a Silver Palace release date.

However, when one is announced, we’ll be sure to update this article, so check back soon for more information.

How to pre-register

To pre-register for Silver Palace, simply follow the instructions outlined below:

  1. Head over to the official Silver Palace website.
  2. Click on the ‘pre-register now’ button.
  3. Enter your email address.
  4. Select the platforms you wish to play on.
  5. Hit ‘pre-register’ to confirm your registration.

Once you have done the above, you will be notified via email when the game is available to download and play.

Platforms

Silver Palace will release on PC, Android, and iOS.

There’s currently no news on whether it will be launching on Xbox Series X/S, PS5, or Switch 2, but we’ll update this section should this change.

Trailers

Silver Palace’s first trailer was revealed on May 13, 2025. It showcases the female protagonist as she walks through the streets of Silvernia, an industrialized metropolis inspired by Victorian-era Britain.

The 10-minute trailer also showcases how players can use a mechanized horse to gallop through environments, while also utilizing a grappling hook to latch onto railings that help speed up traversal. Silver Palace’s traversal mechanics don’t just stop there, though, as the mount can also fly through the air, allowing you to explore the city rooftops.

We also got a great look at the game’s combat, with the MC switching between both melee and ranged attacks. You can see the full trailer above.

Silver Palace gameplay

Like Genshin Impact, Silver Palace enables you to freely switch between four characters, allowing you to unleash powerful skills and combos. There are also quick time events (QTEs), which enable the use of deadly attacks that deal huge amounts of damage.

Characters can also block incoming damage and deliver deadly finishers, with one character shown using a syringe to kill a target and deliver a deadly AoE explosion. Adding to this are stealth sections, where the MC is shown sneaking behind enemies, using takedowns to eliminate and get information from targets.

As we mentioned earlier, traversal is a big part of Silver Palace, with characters having access to a mount that can gallop around the city and fly through the skies above.

According to the official description, Silver Palace gameplay revolves around hunting for clues and battling the factions that inhabit Silvernia.

Story

We currently don’t have too many details regarding Silver Palace’s story, but we know players assume the role of a “detective tasked with investigating crimes and uncovering hidden truths.”

Silvernia is also home to various factions, from corporate monopolies, underground syndicates, royal affiliates, and cults, who are all vying for control of Silverium.

This resource is the key to the city’s industrialization and has “transformed technology and daily life beyond imagination, attracting numerous residents and fostering innovation.”

Players will likely need to stop this “revolutionary” resource from falling into the wrong hands and prevent any disasters.

Silver Palace characters

Argos

A young bartender who has a silver tongue. He joined MISS to be on top, but things didn’t go as planned. Everyone must earn their keep. He now lurks behind the bar, listening and waiting.

Alf

MISS Maid 49. This clueless yet determined girl somehow learned about the detective’s kind nature and chose to stick around. She is blessed with zero common sense, terrible housekeeping skills, and situational unawareness. There is always a new mess under the sun.

Detective (Protagonist)

The protagonist of the game. The detective is rational, reliable, clear-minded, and efficient, and still searches for the truth behind the death of a loved one. After leaving Silvernia for three years following a disaster, the detective has returned to the city.
