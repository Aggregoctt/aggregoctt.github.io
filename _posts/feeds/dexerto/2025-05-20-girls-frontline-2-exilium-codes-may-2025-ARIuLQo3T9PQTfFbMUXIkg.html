How to redeem codes

To redeem codes in Girls’ Frontline 2: Exilium, you can use either the website or claim them in-game. Here’s how to do both:

Method 1

  • Head to the game’s official website.
  • Enter your UID in the first box, then copy and paste a Redeem Code in the second one as seen in the image below.
  • Enter the Verification Code that appears on-screen, and hit the “Redeem” button.

Method 2

  • Once you’ve logged into Girls Frontline 2, click the “Settings” button in the bottom left corner of the game’s main interface
  • Select [Redeem Code] on the bottom right corner of the Personal Information page.
  • Type your code in and hit enter.

It’s important to note that due to possible delays, your rewards will be delivered to your in-game mail within 24 hours of redeeming a code. So, don’t worry if they don’t show up right away.
