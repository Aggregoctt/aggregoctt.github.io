OBSBOT is a renowned name in the webcam world. The brand has gone from strength-to-strength since releasing its first webcam in 2016 and—now in 2025—OBSBOT is the official camera and webcam partner for the Esports World Cup.

OBSBOT is celebrating the partnership by offering Dexerto readers 10% off its entire webcam range. Just head to the official OBSBOT store and use the code DEXERTO10 at checkout (code is valid May 15 to June 13).

OBSBOT Joins the Esports World Cup

The Esport World Cup 2025—AKA the ‘EWC’—takes place in Riyadh in Saudi Arabia, in summer 2025. The 2025 edition features an all-star line-up of popular esports titles including Valorant, League of Legends, Counter-Strike 2, and Apex Legends. Professional teams will go toe-to-toe, duking it out to win a huge cash prize.

For those watching at home, OBSBOT’s Tail 2 cameras will catch every frame and mouse flick in glorious 4K, so you won’t miss a single detail. The cameras will sit around each arena to catch all the action both on the stage and the reactions from the baying crowds set to attend the arena tournaments.

This will provide viewers with the most immersive experience, getting them as close to the arenas as possible for those who can’t be in attendance.

Last year, the Esports World Cup pulled in an astounding 500 million viewers in total, with 2 million heading to Riyadh to catch the action in person.

“Nobody is Nobody”

OBSBOT’s theme for this year is  “Nobody is Nobody”. The messaging is to ensure that every single player is seen, valued, and remembered.

Speaking about the brand’s mission for 2025, Liu Bo, CEO & Founder of OBSBOT, said:

“Every champion begins as a contender. With the Esports World Cup, we’re not only enabling world-class broadcast and streaming, we’re giving every player the chance to shine, and every viewer the chance to witness greatness unfold in real time.”

OBSBOT’s Nobody is Nobody movement seeks to leverage its advanced webcams to the benefit of all players in the tournament, not just the major crowd-drawing teams.

Mohammed Al Nimer, Senior Sales Director of the Esports World Cup Foundation, said of the partnership:

“We are excited to have OBSBOT as our Official Camera and Webcam Partner as we continue to elevate esports experiences worldwide. Their cutting-edge camera technology highlights the power of AI in esports today, capturing the intensity and emotion that defines competition at this level and immersing fans in every moment.”

Putting the Tail 2 to task

OBSBOT’s flagship Tail 2 production camera will be beaming the battles in all their glorious detail during the Esports World Cup 2025.

The Tail 2 is a 4K PTZR live production camera, capable of delivering smooth, cinema-quality shots with very little user input. It also integrates seamlessly with pro-grade broadcasting tech.

To celebrate the collaboration, OBSBOT and EWC will unveil a limited-edition EWC co-branded webcam. For those attending in person, you can visit the exclusive OBSBOT booth at the arena. In this booth you’ll find a recreational area for players and fans to chill. It will be open throughout the event, July 7 through August 30,  offering attendees an engaging space to experience the tournament as it plays out.
