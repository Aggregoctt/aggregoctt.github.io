The long-running soap opera will always have a new instalment waiting for you, and there’s two incredibly easy ways to watch today’s episode of The Bold and the Beautiful.

If Beyond the Gates, Tracker, Elsbeth, and Georgie & Mandy’s First Marriage weren’t enough, the jewel in the CBS crown is its long-running soap opera, The Bold and the Beautiful.

Debuting in 1987, it follows the high society world of Los Angeles fashion and the Forrester family, who deal with divorces, alcoholism, and deaths underneath the industry’s glitz and glamor.

Obviously, trying to catch up with all of the binge-worthy TV show is a bit like climbing a never-ending mountain, but there are two easy ways to catch up with today’s episode of The Bold and the Beautiful.

Watch today’s episode of The Bold and the Beautiful in two ways

If you’ve missed today’s episode of The Bold and the Beautiful as it aired live on CBS, you’ve got two choices – watch it on CBS’ own catch-up, or stream it the following day on Paramount+.

The CBS option is arguably the most straightforward. You’ll need an account on CBS.com alongside an account with a registered cable TV provider. There’s no additional fee here on top of what you already pay, and you’ll have the choice to watch immediate episodes either online or on the app.

If you’re looking for a more long-term solution, it might be worth investing in a Paramount+ subscription. Unlike CBS directly, there’s a bigger back catalog of episodes waiting on the streaming service, ranging from older The Bold and the Beautiful episodes to similar shows and soap operas.

Paramount+ has two plans: an ad-supported Essential plan for $8 a month ($60 a year) and an ad-free plan with Showtime for $13 a month ($120 a year).

Alternatively, Paramount+ offers a seven-day free trial, either through Paramount+ itself or Amazon Prime Video.

The Bold and the Beautiful is broadcast weekdays at 1:30pm ET/12:30pm PT on CBS.
