Popular TikToker Emilie Kiser is receiving an outpouring of support from viewers after her three-year-old son, Trigg Kiser, died from injuries due to an accident in a backyard pool.

Police were called to a home in Chandler, Arizona on the evening of Monday, May 12, to assist with a drowning after Trigg was pulled out of the pool.

Officers began CPR before paramedics arrived on the scene, and Trigg was transported to Chandler Regional Hospital in critical condition. Due to the severe nature of his injuries, the toddler was later flown to Phoenix Children’s Hospital.

On Sunday, May 18, law enforcement confirmed that Trigg had died as a result of the accident.

At the time of writing, a police investigation into the incident is still ongoing, and authorities say they won’t come forward with any further information out of respect to the family until the investigation is complete.
