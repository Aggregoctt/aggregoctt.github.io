Black Ops 6 and Warzone Season 4 brings a Battle Pass full of new weapons, Operator skins, Blueprints, and more.

Season 3 was a Verdansk throwback, but Season 4 looks like it celebrates all things Black Ops. On top of Cold War weapons and Operators returning, we’re also getting the fan-favorite Fringe map.

The majority of these weapons, Operators, and cosmetics arrive through the Season 4 Battle Pass, so here are all the rewards we know of so far.

Price

The Season 3 Battle Pass should cost 1,100 CoD Points ($9.99 / £8.39), as this is how much it always costs.

It includes all of the rewards, you just have to make sure you unlock them all before the season ends (you usually have around 8 weeks). You can pay extra to start a few tiers ahead, but if you want to go the extra mile, you can buy the BlackCell Battle Pass.

The BlackCell pass costs $30 / £25.99, giving you access to the entire Battle Pass and a page of exclusive rewards which offers an extra 1,100 CoD Points.

All skins and weapons so far

The devs have shown off a few of the skins and weapons you can expect to unlock in the Black Ops 6 and Warzone Season 4 Battle Pass, but the full line-up and their tiers will be revealed nearer the time.

Skins

  • Stitch “Supermax” Operator skin (Instant Unlock)
  • Stitch “Supermax BlackCell” Operator skin (BlackCell purchase)
  • Adler “Truth Chaser” Operator skin (Page 12)
  • Adler “Truth Chaser BlackCell” Operator skin (Page 12 + BlackCell Purchase)
  • Stitch “Yard Dog” Operator skin (Completion Page)
  • Stitch “Yard Dog BlackCell” Operator skin (Completion Page + BlackCell purchase)

Weapons

  • FFAR Assault Rifle
  • LC10 SMG
  • Essex Model 07 Marksman Rifle
  • Pickaxe Melee Weapon

It’s worth bearing in mind that these might not be what the weapons are called, as even though they’re the same, Call of Duty tends to change up the names due to reasons like licensing issues.

There’s also a chance they’re not all in the Battle Pass, with one or more being unlocked through an event instead – we’ll keep you posted.
