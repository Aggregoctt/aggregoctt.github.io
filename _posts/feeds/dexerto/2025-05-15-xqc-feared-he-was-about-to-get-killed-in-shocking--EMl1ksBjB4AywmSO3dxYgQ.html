Streaming star xQc described an encounter he had with Amouranth’s husband during an outing with the influencer, saying he genuinely feared for his life.

Kaitlyn ‘Amouranth‘ Siragusa is one of the net’s most popular female streamers — and one of the richest. Because of these two factors, she’s been a target for violent and disturbing behavior from stalkers and thieves.

In fact, her home was broken into by three armed robbers in March 2025, who demanded access to her $20M crypto wallet after she posted about it on social media.

On top of this, Amouranth has experienced a few public falling-outs with her husband, whom she previously claimed was abusive and controlling in a bombshell live stream in 2022.

And in May 2025, the pair had an hours-long argument live on Kick — something fellow streamer and friend to Amouranth, Felix ‘xQc‘ Lengyel, addressed in a stream of his own.

In his broadcast, Lengyel expressed his sympathies for Amouranth, describing an incident where he met her husband that freaked him out so much he thought he was going to die.

xQc says he feared for his life during chilling outing with Amouranth

“There are not a lot of people, it’s getting really late. And then, I swear on everything, the door opens, a guy rolls in a f**king disguise. In a f**king disguise, okay?” he said.

“He pulls up a chair at the table and he sits down, wearing this weird hat and a messenger bag, almost like a thief. He’s like, ‘Guys, I’m recording.’ I’m like, ‘What the f**k?’ And I swear, chat, my blood froze, and I thought I was about to get killed.

“I genuinely thought I was done for. It was GGs. And it was just her husband. It was just… showing up, I guess he has her texts or her phone or her location, whatever, and the guy just pulled up from out of nowhere.”
