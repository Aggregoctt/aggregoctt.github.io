McDonald’s has revealed a new collab with Squishmallows that brings new animal designs to the Happy Meal.

Every few months, McDonald’s comes out with a new collaboration to bring kids all over the United States new toys in their Happy Meals.

The Minecraft Movie meal gave kids new plastic toys featuring their favorite game characters, and now the Golden Arches restaurant is offering a new collab.

For the second time in six months, McDonald’s has partnered with Squishmallows, and fans of the cute plushies are just as excited the second time around to get their hands on some.

McDonald’s new Squishmallow toys are already available

“Squishmallows is at McDonald’s, so you can order a Happy Meal for the kid you totally have. Sure,” said the McDonald’s Senior Marketing Director in a post on X.

There are 12 new creatures to collect, and fans are excited to head to their local store to get their hands on them.

Fans flocked to social media to share their thoughts, with many people revealing their excitement for the new Happy Meal Toys.

“Don’t call me out like this, dog, chill. (I ordered two meals today for lunch),” one user commented.

“When are they releasing them. One of my kids favorite toys besides Pokémon cards,” a second user asked.

A third replied: “OMG, these Squishmallows are too cute! Gotta grab a Happy Meal ASAP… Haley’s my fave!”

All McDonald’s Squishmallows Happy Meal Toys

Here are all 12 Squishmallows you can get in a McDonald’s Happy Meal:

  • Halley (Intergalactic Axolotl)
  • Maritza (Cactus)
  • Rorty (Monster)
  • Stevon (Golden Retriever)
  • Cookie (Flamingo)
  • Junie (Banana)
  • Cam (Cat)
  • Rossi (Cheetah)
  • Mirren (Moth)
  • Babs (Blu Jay)
  • Zumirez (Bat)
  • Charles (Pickle)

This comes just weeks after McDonald’s launched the McCrispy Strips, its new chicken tender option, as it gets ready to release Snack Wraps again after years of fans asking for it to return.
