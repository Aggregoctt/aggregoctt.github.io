Jake Paul is set to go up against Julio Cesar Chavez Jr in a boxing match, and a $1M ticket to the event has been purchased.

Back in April, Paul announced that he is going up against Chavez Jr this summer in a pay-per-view boxing match on Saturday, June 28.

Tickets almost immediately went on sale for those looking to watch it in person, and one package was made available that cost a staggering $1M to purchase.

Called the ‘Owner’s Experience,’ many were certain that the high-priced ticket wasn’t going to sell before the event happened. However, on Wednesday, May 14, it did.

Lawfirm spends $1M on Jake Paul ‘Owner’s Experience’ ticket

According to Paul’s promotion company, Most Valuable Promotions, the ticket went to the Chicago-based Torklaw personal injury law firm.

The ‘Owner’s Experience’ package will give Torklaw “unparalleled seats, access, and amenities” during fight week and on fight now.

Here’s everything included with the $1,000,000 ticket:

  • Private owner’s box inside corral for two plus four front-row ringside seats
  • Private green room with all-inclusive food and top-shelf open bar 
  • VIP access to open workout, press conference, and weigh-in
  • Meet and Greets with main card fighters
  • Exclusive fight week merchandise bag and fight poster signed by Jake Paul and Julio Cesar Chavez Jr.
  • Full TorkLaw brand integration into the DAZN pay-per-view broadcast 
  • Full event IP rights

Thanks to the sale, MVP has partnered with another company to offer three additional VIP ringside ticket packages.

For $6,500, you’ll get access to a front row ringside seat at the event in the Emerald VIP package. If that’s a bit too rich for your blood, the $4,250 Diamond VIP package is right behind that. In the third row, you’ll be able to buy the Platinum VIP package for just $2,500.

All three packages will have a VIP arena entrance to bypass long lines, hospitality service, and VIP seating for the events. The Diamond and Emerald VIP packages receive an in-ring photo opportunity after the fight, and the latter gets a fight poster signed by Jake Paul himself.
