An Ultra Wormhole is opening up in Pokemon TCG Pocket to welcome the Ultra Beasts with a new set called Extradimensional Crisis, adding over 100 new cards to the game.

Pokemon TCG Pocket’s meta is about to change once more as visitors from Ultra Space are entering the game with a new expansion that will put new strategies on the table.

Just like with Mythical Island, Triumphant Light, and Shining Revelry, the upcoming Extradimensional set will be short, with just over 100 cards that will include Ultra Beasts in regular, ex, and full-art cards. Plus, the debut of the strangest Legendary, Type: Null, and so much more.

Here are all of the cards that will release on May 29, 2025, with the Extradimensional Crisis expansion.

Extradimensional Crisis full booster pack card list

The Extradimensional Crisis set will include normal, ex, Trainer, and secret rare cards. However, the specific number of cards hasn’t been confirmed yet.
