You’re not going to want to miss The Handmaid’s Tale Season 6 Episode 9, ‘Execution’, so here’s exactly what time to expect it on Hulu, as well as details on the upcoming episodes. 

Given Season 6 is the last ever chapter of the Hulu series, these final episodes are significant. Up until now, Commander Lawrence and Serena have been heading up Gilead reforms via New Bethlehem, but the former has switched sides after realizing his fellow High Commanders are using him to lure US refugees back. 

As for June, she’s been working with Luke, Moira, and Tuello to enact Mayday and the US government-in-exile’s plan to take down Gilead. Although their dry run at Jezebels didn’t go to plan, June and Lawrence joined forces and devised a new objective: target Serena and Commander Wharton’s wedding. 

All of this is building up to the revolution teased in The Handmaid’s Tale Season 6 trailer. Who will come out on top is anyone’s guess, but so you don’t miss out, here’s when to expect the penultimate chapter. 

The ninth episode of The Handmaid’s Tale Season 6 will land on Hulu on May 20, 2025, at 12am ET. This means those on PT got it a day earlier – May 19, 2025, at 9pm. 

If you live outside of the US, you either have to wait for an international release, or sign up to Hulu by using a VPN and purchasing a gift card for the streaming service. If you go with the latter, here’s when to expect the new instalment in different timezones:

  • 1am Brazil
  • 5am UK
  • 6am Central European Summer Time
  • 9:30am India Standard Time
  • 2pm Australia
  • 4pm New Zealand
