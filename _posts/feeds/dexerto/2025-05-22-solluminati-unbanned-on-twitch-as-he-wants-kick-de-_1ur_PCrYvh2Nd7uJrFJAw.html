Streamer SoLLUMINATI has been unbanned by Twitch after releasing a YouTube video about his most recent ban, and threatening to leave for KICK. 

Back in early May, SoLLUMINATI was unbanned by Twitch after having been banned from the Amazon-owned platform for five years. Despite a successful return to the platform, he has already copped two bans. 

His first, which came on May 4, prompted Twitch to hand him a test about “hateful” conduct. SoLLUMINATI claimed he passed that with flying colors and didn’t get any of the questions wrong. His ban, however, came on May 20, shortly after he had close to 90,000 viewers during an NBA 2K wage stream. 

As per Twitch’s procedures, they haven’t shared the reason for the ban. However, SoLLUMINATI has since been unbanned, and it came after he made a YouTube video threatening to leave for KICK. 

SoLLUMINATI urges KICK to sign him up from Twitch

“This time, I really don’t know why I got banned. We all seen it, I have no clue why I got banned,” he said in his video. “The only thing I can say is they seen that I hit 87,000 viewers. I don’t know what I did, y’all tell me.”

The streamer repeated that he doesn’t “feel safe” on Twitch now that he has two bans in a month, and called on Adin Ross to sign him up to Kick. 

“Adin, KICK, y’all time has come. I just had 87,000 viewers, it is time for y’all to spoil me. KICK, I think we’ve got to bring the culture and drama to KICK, y’all.”

Adin Ross ‘welcomes’ SoLLUMINATI to KICK

It didn’t take long for Ross to “welcome” SoLLUMINATI to the Stake-backed platform. “Chat, SoLLUMINATI is going to come to KICK. It is happening. It is happening sooner than we all think,” he confirmed. “SoLLUMINATI, welcome to KICK.”

Adin also addressed SoLLUMINATI’s viewers who don’t want him to make the jump, questioning if they want him to be “held back” by Twitch’s bans. 

SoLLUMINATI also revealed that he is scrapping a number of big IRL streams that he had planned to host on Twitch following the new suspension. 

“Twitch don’t even deserve me, man. I’m talking about bringing IRLs to Twitch and all this s**t? Man, y’all don’t even deserve me, man.”
