Top 20 list in 2025 ---

Fortnite‘s pickaxe collection is legendary, and it’s no surprise, coming from a game that has Adventure Time, Star Wars, and Marvel cosmetics. However, some of them are much rarer than others.

With so many pickaxes coming in and out of the shop and exclusive rewards tied to limited-time promotions, how do you know which ones are the rarest?

From special collaborations to challenging tasks, here’s the ultimate list of the 20 rarest pickaxes in Fortnite.

Rarest pickaxes in Fortnite

1. Blade of Champions

  • Release date: Nov 13, 2024

It’s a glowing, futuristic sword with sharp, angular edges and a fiery energy coming off of it. Only a select few players have unlocked this legendary pickaxe after winning Fortnite’s FCS tournament. It’s super exclusive, making it the rarest pickaxe out there. If you got it, you know you’re among the elite.

2. Axe of Champions

  • Release date: May 10, 2025

This OG version has a slick, gold design, making it even more majestic than the 2.0 one. You can’t get it, though, as it was replaced by the 2.0 version. This means that, unless you won a tournament before Chapter 6 Season 3, you will not be able to wield this trophy.

3. Merry Mint Axe

Release date: Nov 6, 2019

The Merry Mint Axe features a bright, candy-cane style with red, white, and green stripes. This one’s a fan favorite, no doubt. It’s a re-skin of the Candy Axe, but the catch is that getting it was way harder, as you had to buy Fortnite merch at GameStop for a chance to snag it.

4. Velocity Edge

  • Release date: Jul 3, 2023

A sleek, futuristic design with neon colors, resembling the clean and smooth edges of a jet fighter. Exclusively for players using Amazon Luna, the Velocity Edge is super rare since not many people use Luna to play Fortnite. If you’re lucky enough to have it, keep it in your collection because it might never return.

5. Wavecrest

  • Release date: Nov 14, 2020

It’s a clean, silver-blue design with wave-like patterns. It almost looks like it could belong to a pirate ship, but it’s sleek enough for a tech genius. If you bought an Intel CPU back in 2020, you could’ve unlocked the Wavecrest pickaxe. It was one of the rarest Intel exclusives and hasn’t been seen since.

6. The Destroyer’s Destroyer

  • Release date: Mar 10, 2023

A powerful, dark, and techy design with sharp edges and a glowing, futuristic vibe. This bad boy was only available to GeForce Now users who logged in during a specific time window. As one of the rare Nvidia-exclusive pickaxes, the Destroyer is one for the hardcore fans who played on mobile.

7. Crowbar

  • Release date: Jul 11, 2018

This pickaxe is exactly what you’d expect: simple, strong, and classic. Think of it as a crowbar straight from an action movie, ready for whatever Fortnite throws at it. The Crowbar is the definition of limited edition. Available only for a few days during the High Stakes challenges, it’s become one of Fortnite’s hardest-to-find pickaxes.

8. Frozen Axe

  • Release date: Dec 6, 2018

Icy blue and white colors make this pickaxe sparkle, with frosty shards dangling from the edges. It’s like holding a glacier in your hands. A reskin of the Crimson Axe, the Frozen Axe was obtainable by completing challenges during the 14 Days of Fortnite event. It’s loud, icy, and so rare now.

9. Lucky Lance

  • Release date: Jul 21, 2022

The Lucky Lance has a clean, gold aesthetic with a four-leaf clover design on the top, perfect for those who love good fortune. This one is a throwback to Chapter 3’s summer event, and despite not being the most popular pickaxe when it dropped, it’s now one of the rarest.

10. AC/DC

  • Release date: Dec 14, 2017

This electric guitar-inspired pickaxe crackles with lightning and energy, making it look like it’s about to rock the entire Fortnite island. As one of the highest-tier rewards from the oldest Battle Pass, ACDC is by far the rarest Battle Pass pickaxe you can own. If you have it, you can really flex those OG vibes.

11. Twin Talons

  • Release date: Dec 14, 2020

A sharp, sleek dual-blade design with a beautiful indigo color that makes them look both dangerous and elegant. The Twin Talons are a dual-wielded pickaxe, available to players who have completed the Reboot a Friend challenges. These beauties are part of the Indigo Kuno set, and like all tournament-related rewards, they’re rare.

12. Marshy Smasher

  • Release date: Dec 6, 2018

It’s a fluffy, musical hammer inspired by Marshmello’s mascot. Think a soft, fun vibe with a serious hit. The Marshy Smasher pickaxe was awarded for participating in the Marshmello live event challenges. It’s got a special golden style too, making it even harder to get.

13. Leviathan Axe

  • Release date: Dec 4, 2020

This axe has a massive, sharp design with intricate Norse markings and a glowing red aura. It’s like you’re wielding a piece of mythology. Who doesn’t want to swing Kratos’ iconic Leviathan Axe? It hasn’t been available for almost 5 years, and fans are still clamoring for it to return.

14. Octane Whomper

  • Release date: Dec 13, 2021

Resembling a wrench, this pickaxe has a mechanical vibe with a racing-themed design, reminding you of fast cars and high-speed thrills. A perfect crossover for Rocket League fans, the Octane Womper pickaxe is a rare gem that you could only unlock through Rocket League challenges.

15. Controller

  • Release date: Sep 11, 2018

A sleek, modern gaming controller design with a glowing finish. It’s simple, but it screams “gamer” in the best way. The Controller pickaxe was part of the first PlayStation Plus pack, and it’s considered one of the rarest exclusive pickaxes in Fortnite history. If you missed out on it, you’re probably never going to see it again.

16. Indigo Inverter

  • Release date: Nov 22, 2024

A glowing, holographic design with subtle purple and blue hues. It looks like something from the future. This one’s for the PS5 owners out there. You had to buy a PS5 to get it, and now, this item’s super rare.

17. Resonator

  • Release date: Sep 27, 2018

The Resonator is a futuristic, dark design with angular lines and glowing accents that reflect Xbox’s iconic look. Exclusively for Xbox owners, the Resonator pickaxe is a rare gem tied to specific console bundles. It’s pretty much impossible to find nowadays, making it a true collector’s item.

18. Electri-Claw

  • Release date: Jun 4, 2021

Sleek, sharp, and super high-tech, the Electra Claw has a fierce aesthetic with electric blue details and a polished finish. Part of the Nintendo Switch bundle, the Electra Claw is a rare pickaxe that only a handful of players will ever get their hands on.

19. Diamond Jack

  • Release date: Apr 22, 2020

A sparkling, jewel-encrusted axe with vibrant neon colors and an aura of cosmic energy. This one’s super rare now. Travis Scott’s Diamond Jack pickaxe hasn’t been available in the shop for over 1,700 days. It’s a reminder of the legendary Astronomical event!

20. Midnight Scythe

  • Release date: Mar 18, 2024

The Midnight Scythe is next-level rare. This spooky, sleek beauty was only available to players who redeemed a special V-Bucks card between March 18th and April 14th of 2024. It’s like holding a piece of Fortnite history, and because it was tied to such a limited promotion, it’s now one of the hardest pickaxes to get your hands on.
