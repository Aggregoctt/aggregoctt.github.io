The FFAR Assault Rifle returns in Black Ops 6 and Warzone Season 4, and it’s set to be an excellent sniper support option in battle royale.

The FAMAS has been in Call of Duty for a long time, and although traditionally a 3-round burst, Black Ops tends to offer it as a fully automatic option. Cold War’s FFAR was a dominant choice in both multiplayer and Warzone, with its fast fire rate and low recoil lending itself to a sniper support option.

Now called the FFAR 1, it returns in the Season 4 update on May 29, so here’s everything you need to know about unlocking it.

How to get FFAR 1 in BO6 and Warzone

You need to complete Page 6 in the Season 4 Battle Pass to unlock the FFAR 1 Warzone and Black Ops 6. If you don’t manage to unlock it by the end of Season 4, it’ll be available as an Armory unlock.

The new Assault Rifle is completely free, so don’t worry about having to shell out for the Battle Pass just to unlock it. However, buying the Battle Pass will unlock a Blueprint for it on Page 11.

It’s been described as a fully automatic Assault Rifle with a “very high rate of fire and excellent CQB power, but slower handling and mobility.”

Although it has a “blazing fast fire rate that rivals the SMG weapon class” and a rapid TTK, it also has a lot more recoil than a traditional AR. This means that once again, it’s set to be a fantastic sniper support option, dealing with enemies at close-mid-range while you use your Sniper Rifle for anything further away.

The Season 4 update also brings the LC10 SMG, another powerful gun from Black Ops Cold War, that has “excellent accuracy and range, with a moderate rate of fire,” and Black Ops 4’s Essex Model 87 Marksman Rifle arrives through the John Wick spinoff, Ballerina, event.
