Newly-released GTA 6 screenshots are hinting at the return of a character from a forgotten Rockstar franchise.

Rockstar Games shocked the world with the debut of GTA 6’s second trailer, along with a series of screenshots, location details and character bios.

As expected, the internet has been hard at work picking apart every frame of the trailer, and they’ve already made some pretty wild discoveries.

Interestingly, one character’s tattoos have fans extremely excited, because it appears that Piggsy from Manhunt will be making an appearance of some sort in VI.

GTA 6 screenshots hint at Piggsy’s return following Manhunt

Piggsy was an antagonist from Rockstar’s extremely controversial psychological horror game Manhunt.

The game, which was banned in numerous countries, features a pig head-wearing cannibalistic mass murderer named Piggsy.

As noted by YouTube channel GTA VI O’Clock, screenshots of the character Cal Hampton show that he has what appears to be a Piggsly tattoo on his right leg.

Hampton’s character bio reveals that he’s a conspiracy theorist who “snoops on Coast Guard comms with a few beers and some private browser tabs open.”

However, as GTA VI O’Clock notes: “The actual Piggsy from Manhunt is highly unlikely to appear in GTA 6, since Rockstar’s grisly action adventure was set in Carcer City in 2003, and he’d be less of a threat, having introduced his arms to a chainsaw and fallen into a very deep pit.”

That said, Manhunt takes place in Carcer City and is technically part of the GTA 3D Universe. The 3D Universe spans numerous games, but doesn’t include GTA 4, GTA 5, or GTA 6.

As such, it’s possible for Piggsy to still be alive in the world of GTA 6. There could even be a copycat killer. Or, you know, it could just be a neat reference/Easter Egg.

Rockstar loves to include mysterious serial killer side missions in its games, such as Edmund Lowry Jr in RDR2 or The Infinity Killer in GTA 5, so there’s a good chance we get something similar with VI.
