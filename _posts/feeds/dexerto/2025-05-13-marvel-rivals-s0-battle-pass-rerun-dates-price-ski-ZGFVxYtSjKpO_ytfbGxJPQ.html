You asked. You begged. You may have even rage-posted about it. And now, the Marvel Rivals devs have finally listened: the Season 0 Battle Pass is making a comeback.

Whether you missed it the first time or just want to flex on the newbies with that sweet Galactic Claw Black Panther skin, this is your moment. But don’t sleep on it; this rerun is only sticking around for two weeks, and it’s got zero free stuff.

Here’s everything you need to know about the Marvel Rivals rerun of the S0 Battle Pass.

This limited-time event runs from May 15, 2025, at 2:00 AM PDT through May 29, 2025, at 2:00 AM PDT.

The return coincides with the “Galacta’s Gift” event, which celebrates the community by offering free in-game rewards. However, the S0 Battle Pass itself remains a separate, premium offering.

S0 Battle Pass price

The returning S0 Battle Pass is available for 590 Lattice, which translates to roughly $4.99 to $7 USD, depending on regional pricing.

This price point is a special anniversary discount, and unlike current seasonal passes, the S0 rerun offers no free track. All rewards within this pass are classified as Luxury Rewards, meaning you must purchase the pass to unlock any content.

NetEase has also confirmed that any future Battle Pass reruns will happen at least two seasons apart and will cost a minimum of 20% more than the original price.
