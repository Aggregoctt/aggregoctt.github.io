What’s it about?

The three-part docu-series follows the tragic true story of the Bakersfield 3 – a group of young friends whose disappearances and death rocked a California community in 2018.

James Kulstad was murdered, while Micah Holsonbake and Baylee Despot vanished, all within weeks of each other. It initially seemed like a series of unrelated crimes, but as their grieving mothers began to dig deeper, they uncovered connections between their children.

A Tale of Murder and Motherhood charts their tireless efforts for justice. As per the official synopsis, “In the spring of 2018 over the course of just 34 days, a young woman and man went missing, and another young man was murdered in Bakersfield, California.
