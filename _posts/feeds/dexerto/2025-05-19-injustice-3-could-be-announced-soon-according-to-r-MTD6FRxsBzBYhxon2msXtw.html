It’s been eight years (bet you feel old now) since we were blessed with Injustice 2, one of the best DC games ever. Now, thanks to a subtle tease by a renowned dataminer, a third game could be on the horizon.

Produced by Warner Bros Games, Injustice is one of their most renowned franchises, standing alongside Hogwarts Legacy, Back 4 Blood, and the Batman: Arkham series. Just like Arkham, Injustice is a superhero game, letting you battle against Wonder Woman, Batman, Blue Beetle, Superman, and a plethora of different heroes.

Unfortunately, it’s been eight years since an Injustice game was released, with no word on whether we’d ever get a third. That was until a renowned dataminer known for their reliable predictions revealed a subtle hint about Injustice 3.

Injustice 3 could be coming soon

The account MultiverserSusie on X is renowned for accurately revealing Warner Bros Games news before they’re fully announced.

In a subtle post on X, they explained, “MultiVersus shutting down is an injustice, then doing it all again is another injustice. Leaving me without any leaks is yet again another injustice.” Eagleyed fans were quick to spot the three mentions of Injustice and quickly began speculating.

On the surface, this can seem like some relatively loose speculation, but the hint was only strengthened by the account’s follow-up reply, which simply read “3.” Three mentions of Injustice can only mean one thing — they’re teasing the upcoming announcement of Injustice 3.
