After recently arriving on PlayStation, Forza Horizon 5 isn’t slowing down as players will be able to get a Lamborghini Revuelto as a new reward car.

Forza Horizon 5 has always been a beautiful game. Driving through the streets of Mexico to your next race is mesmerizing, especially if you’re doing it at high speed with your friends.

That high speed is about to get even more attractive thanks to Forza Horizon 5’s collaboration with Lamborghini, revealing a jaw-dropping livery design that every player will want to get their hands on.

How to get Revuelto Forza Horizon Edition

For players looking to bag this latest addition, the Lamborghini Revuelto Forza Horizon Special Livery can be unlocked for free by heading to Horizon Realms, a new feature that allows you to explore 12 Evolving World locations and a variety of new races.

Once you get there, head to the Horizon Stadium Circuit (12th Evolving World) and win the race. When you do, you’ll unlock the exclusive Lamborghini Revuelto Forza Horizon Special Livery.

However, unlocking it is just the start. If you want it to look like the Forza Horizon Special Livery, which is free for everyone across all platforms, then you’ll need to download the paint job via the Share Code.

So, once you’ve unlocked the car, follow the steps below:

  1. Head to your House or Outpost.
  2. Open the Garage tab.
  3. Select ‘Designs & Paint.’
  4. Choose ‘Find New Designs.’
  5. Enter the following Share Code: 687 884 132

With that, the stunning new design for the Lamborghini Revuelto will be unlocked, allowing you to jump in and be the talk of Mexico.
