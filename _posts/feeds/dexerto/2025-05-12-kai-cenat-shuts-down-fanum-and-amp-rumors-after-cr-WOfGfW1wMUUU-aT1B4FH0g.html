Kai Cenat has shut down claims that Fanum left AMP after the Twitch star made several posts that led fans to believe he had.

In early May, Fanum’s social media posts on X and Snapchat heavily suggested he had left AMP. He claimed they unfollowed him and removed his access to the account in one post, and followed it up with other comments.

“They sold they soul, they mad I won’t do it,” he said on Snapchat.

Fanum added on Twitter: “They forget all the sh*t that I did, I was feeding the team when I didn’t have sh*t. I was taking a risk, I done got all of us rich.”

After these posts, Fanum went silent, not addressing the furor that he had caused, with fans concerned there had been a falling out in the group. However, fellow member Kai Cenat has shut down the speculation entirely.

Kai Cenat addresses Fanum departure rumors

“Let’s go ahead and address the elephant in the room,” Kai said during his May 10 stream. “Y’all need to be studied. How the f**k yall get bamboozled into thinking Fanum left AMP? Everybody who fell for Fanum leaving AMP, I’m not going to lie… that just goes to show how much y’all actually watch [us].

“You being on X and looking at captions or looking at a video that’s clipped out of context… you can’t always believe that sh*t. Especially on X, that’s where it stems from.”

He added: “Did you hear Fanum out of his mouth say ‘I’m out’? Did you hear any of us say that? No, bro, you can’t always believe shit on the internet.”

Kai also shared that he was hanging out with Fanum, and the AMP star was “confused” about the rumors going around social media.
