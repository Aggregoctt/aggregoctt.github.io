Call of Duty: Warzone fans have, again, praised the battle royale’s casual mode but believe it still needs a big change to be made to it.

When Warzone first launched in 2020 as a part of Modern Warfare, it had the main battle royale mode, and Plunder. Since then, limited-time modes have become part and parcel of things. 

Modern Warfare actually had some of the best, including Iron Trials, Vehicle Royale, and Mini Royale. There have been events for Godzilla and King Kong at times over the years, and even Resurgence overtook the main mode in popularity at times. 

However, it’s Black Ops 6’s take on things with Battle Royale Casual that has earned the most praise of any since Iron Trials. As the name suggests, it is a much more casual battle royale experience and removes a lot of the stress of a usual Warzone game. And, while fans love it, they’d also like some changes. 

Warzone players praise casual battle royal as “best thing”

That’s right, the mode has been rightfully praised again as one of the “best things” that could have ever been added, but players would love a few tweaks. 

“Just need a solos playlist on it. Also, in quads, it’d be nice if the bots were squadded together,” one fan said. “Solos for sure. Even Duos would be manageable,” another agreed. 

“I agree it’s beautiful, I wish there was one with a little bit better bots, though. The current ones are not even a threat to your plates,” another suggested. “I would like to see them add how many real players you’ve killed on the scoreboard,” another chimed in. 

Other players noted the inclusion of the casual mode “makes for less easy players to fight in regular BR,” and that has a knock-on effect with Ranked play too.

Obviously, only the devs can make tweaks to the mode, and it’d be interesting to see if they do. It is clearly popular.
