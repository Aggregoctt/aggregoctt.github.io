How to redeem codes

Redeeming codes in Fantasy Life i isn’t too tricky, as all you need to do is load up the game and choose your first life by heading to the Guild Office. Once you’ve done that, follow the steps below:

  • Open up your Weird Tablet.
  • Head to the Gifts Icon.
  • Copy and paste your code.
  • Choose Accept.

With that, your rewards will be available to collect. It’s worth noting that these codes are likely case sensitive, so we recommend copying and pasting the code into your game.

On top of this, all codes will require an internet connection to redeem, so ensure you have one before inputting the codes. If there’s an error, simply try again.
