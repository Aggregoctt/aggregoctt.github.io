Overwatch 2 players can participate in a new Street Fighter 6 event to earn a bunch of free rewards and here’s a list of them all.

It’s safe to say Blizzard has been ramping up the amount of collabs lately, as not only have they brought back a second wave of LE SSERAFIM skins, but in this season alone, we’ve already gotten crossovers for Gundam Wing as well as Street Fighter.

The latter has just dropped along with the May 20 patch, giving a total of eight heroes completely new skins based on some of the characters from the popular fighting game franchise, making it the biggest crossover so far in the history of Overwatch 2.

As a way to keep things even more exciting, there’s also a limited-time event to commemorate the collab, allowing you to obtain Street Fighter-related cosmetics for free.

Overwatch 2 Street Fighter 6 event schedule

The Street Fighter event 6 went live on May 20, 2025, and it will run until June 3, 2025. This means you have two weeks to participate in it to earn rewards.

As usual, there’s no telling whether or not these rewards will be available again in the future, so make sure to jump into the game if you’d like to snatch them and don’t want to miss out.

All Street Fighter 6 event challenges & rewards

Nine challenges have been made available as part of this event, each having different rewards. A lot of these are quite simple, only requiring you to play a certain number of games, use ultimates, and deal damage, among others.

As for the rewards, aside from XP, you’ll be able to get a weapon charm, voice lines for Sigma, Widow, and Hanzo, as well as sprays.
