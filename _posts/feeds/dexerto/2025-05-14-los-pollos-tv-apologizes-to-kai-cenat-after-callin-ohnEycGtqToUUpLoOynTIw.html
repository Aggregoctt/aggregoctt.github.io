Kai Cenat not only received an apology from Los Pollos TV and his dad after he called Streamer University a “scam.” However, Kai also a pretty big offer from them.

Back in February, Kai Cenat revealed he had plans for one of his biggest streams yet, as he wanted to bring hundreds of content creators together at his own Streamer University. The Twitch star also wanted to get big names like MrBeast and Mark Rober involved to teach some of the classes. 

Fast forward a few months, and the plan is almost a reality. Kai has got a university on board – but he won’t reveal where it is going to be – and has opened up the application process to content creators. 

He was, however, heartbroken when Los Pollos TV’s dad claimed that the whole thing was going to be a “scam” and a “money grab” that was comparable to stand-up comedy lessons. 

Los Pollos TV’s dad apologizes to Kai Cenat

Since then, Los Pollos’ dad has issued an apology to Kai, and even extended an olive branch with an offer to be involved with Streamer University.

“Kai, you know, man. It’s a free thing that he’s doing. When he said he was heartbroken, it upset me because he’s only said nice things about me. I always liked this kid. Every time we spoke, he’s cool,” Los’ dad said. 

“I’m throwing this out there, because of what was said and the apology I just made, again, I feel terrible. If you’d like us to come in and give a lecture or seminar, the two of us will be there.”

The AMP star has been reviewing applications for both students and faculty members at his Streamer University, with MrBeast offering to teach attendees about YouTube. 

Kai confirmed that the line-up of those involved will be announced “soon,” and it’ll be interesrting to see who makes the cut.
