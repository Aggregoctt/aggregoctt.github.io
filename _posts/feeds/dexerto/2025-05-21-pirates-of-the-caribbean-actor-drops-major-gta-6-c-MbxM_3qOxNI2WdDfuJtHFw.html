A Spanish actor from Pirates of the Caribbean and Rambo: Last Blood has appeared to confirm his involvement in GTA 6 after a cryptic deleted post.

Ever since GTA 6 was first announced, players have been trying to figure out the game’s actors.

While no one has been confirmed just yet, in May, Rockstar revealed the second GTA 6 trailer, along with some huge info about VI and new screenshots.

The game’s website confirmed several new characters alongside protagonists Jason and Lucia. And although we still don’t know for sure who is playing those two, a deleted Instagram post may have revealed the identity of an important co-star.

Raul Bautista’s GTA 6 actor may have just been confirmed

One of GTA 6’s big characters will be Raul Bautista, a seasoned bank robber.

Bautista’s section on Rockstar’s website includes a screenshot of him engaging in a Heat-like police shootout alongside Jason and Lucia.

Bautista could very well end up being one of VI’s antagonists, as his bio description states: “Raul’s recklessness raises the stakes with every score. Sooner or later, his crew will have to double down or pull their chips from the table.”

Well, on May 20, Spanish actor Oscar Jaenada from movies such as Che, Pirates of the Caribbean: On Stranger Tides, The Shallows, and Rambo: Last Blood made a very interesting Instagram post.

As noted by GameRoll on X, a user who had previously leaked information about VI, Jaenada posted a shirt with Raul’s face on it to his now-deleted Instagram story.

“I think it’s him. Pretty insane to be posting that under a Rockstar NDA lol,” GameRoll remarked.

Jaenada’s look definitely matches that of Bautista. While this may not be a full confirmation, the post certainly raises quite a lot of speculation with many users now convinced that he’s in GTA 6.

“Pretty hard to argue with that one if he posted that,” one user said.

“It’s definitely him! This was probably intentional, or he didn’t understand how the GTA community works and didn’t take Rockstar’s advice seriously,” another chimed in.

“IT’S THE SPANIARD FROM POTC4, JASON AND LUCIA ARE COOKED,” someone else claimed.
