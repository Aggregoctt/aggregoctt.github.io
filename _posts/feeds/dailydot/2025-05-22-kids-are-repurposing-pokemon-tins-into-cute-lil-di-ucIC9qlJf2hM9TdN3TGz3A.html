Here’s how to make your own Pokémon jewelry box: 

  1. Gather supplies—any empty Pokémon tin, Scissors, double sided tape, fabric marker or gel pen, ruler, velvet fabric, chipboard or thin cardboard like a cereal box, felt, a lighter, and a hot glue gun.
  2. Cut 4 felt rectangles to 4.5 inches by 2.75 inches for the ring rolls.
  3. Tightly roll the felt into tubes and hot glue them along the long edge. Repeat this 4 times.
  4. Cut two 4 ½ by 2 ⅔ cardboard panels to fit the top and bottom of the case, and set them aside. 
  5. Wrap fabric around the ring rolls and glue them closed.
  6. Tape fabric to one side of each of the cardboard panels, and wrap it around to secure it on the reverse side. Only one flat side of each panel will be visible.
  7. Hot glue the fabric-wrapped bottom panel into the bottom half of the tin.
  8. Hot glue the ring rolls into the bottom half of the tin on top of the fabric-wrapped panel.
  9. Measure and wrap a cardboard divider to separate the ring rolls from the open compartment.
  10. Hot glue the divider into the bottom half of the tin.
  11. Hot glue the fabric-wrapped top panel into the top half of the tin.
