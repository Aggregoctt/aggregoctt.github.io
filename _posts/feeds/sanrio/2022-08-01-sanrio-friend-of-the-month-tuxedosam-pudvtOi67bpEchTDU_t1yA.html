Sanrio Friend of the Month: Tuxedosam

August is finally here! Let's learn more about our Friend of the Month, Tuxedosam!

Tuxedosam is a fashionable penguin who loves to eat. He has studied abroad in Britain and has a collection of 365 bow ties. 

Check out more fun facts about our Friend of the Month:

Let's meet Tuxedosam's siblings!

Pam and Tam are Tuxedosam's younger brothers. Pam is a curious and energetic little fellow, who’s always the life of the party! He loves to plan fun and exciting things to do and collect hats. Tam loves to sleep. He’s a bit on the shy side, but knows how to lighten the mood with his friendly comments. His pajama collection rivals no other!

Let's meet Tuxedosam's best friend!

Chip is a friendly seal who is Tuxedosam's real life side kick!
