Sanrio Friend of the Month: Hello Kitty

November is finally here! Say hello to our Friend of the Month, Hello Kitty!

Hello Kitty was born in the suburbs of London and lives with her twin sister Mimmy and her parents. Her hobbies include baking cookies and making new friends. She is about 5 apples tall but weighs 3 apples! Her dream is to become a pianist or a poet someday. As she always says, "You can never have too many friends!". 

Check out more fun facts about our Friend of the Month:

Let’s meet Hello Kitty’s twin sister, Mimmy!

Mimmy is Hello Kitty’s twin sister! She has a shy and quiet personality and wears a yellow bow on her right ear. Her favorite subject is home economics and she’s great at embroidery and patchwork!

Let’s meet Hello Kitty’s parents and grandparents!

Papa has a hilarious sense of humor and is the backbone of the family. Mama is kind, caring, and is famous for her apple pie. Grandpa loves painting and telling stories about his past. Grandma makes a delicious pudding and loves nothing more than sitting in her rocking chair doing embroidery.

Let’s meet Hello Kitty’s childhood friend and boyfriend, Dear Daniel!

Daniel Starr is Hello Kitty’s boyfriend! His favorite hobbies are animal photography, dancing, and playing piano. He hopes to become a professional cameraman someday.

Let’s meet Hello Kitty’s best friend, Tiny Chum!

Tiny Chum is one of six brothers and sisters and is a little rascal. He lives with Hello Kitty and wears a ribbon that she gifted him!

Let’s meet Hello Kitty’s friends!

Hello Kitty has too many friends to count! She has close relationships with all of her friends of different shapes and sizes.
