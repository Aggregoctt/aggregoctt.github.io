Hello Capricorn Season

It’s finally Capricorn season! (December 22 - January 19)

Capricorn is a sign that represents time and responsibility, and are typically independent beings. They are very hard workers and will always strive to reach their personal and professional goals. Capricorns possess an inner ambition which equips them to handle anything life throws at them!

Let’s learn more about our Capricorn friends!

Capricorn Strengths:

Super organized, loves sleep, honest, great leadership

Capricorn Likes:

Family, tradition, music, gardening, reading
