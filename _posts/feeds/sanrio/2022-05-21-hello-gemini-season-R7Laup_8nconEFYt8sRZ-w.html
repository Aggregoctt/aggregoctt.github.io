Hello Gemini Season

It’s officially Gemini season! (May 21 to June 20)

Gemini's changeable and open mind makes them excellent artists, especially writers and journalists, and their skills and flexibility make them shine in team sports. Gemini’s are fun loving, and are born with a wish to experience everything there is out there, in the world. And they are the cheerleaders of the friend group, always there to inspire and cheer up a friend in need.

Let’s learn more about our Gemini friends!

Gemini Strengths:

Social butterfly, affectionate, curious, adaptable, loves to learn new things

Gemini Likes:

Making plans with friends, writing, traveling, team sports, art, and reading
