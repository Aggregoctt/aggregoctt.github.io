Sanrio Friend of the Month: Little Twin Stars

It’s finally December! Say hello to our Friend of the Month, Little Twin Stars!

Kiki and Lala were born on the Omoiyari Star in the Yume Star-Cloud and were sent to Earth by their parents to shine as brightly as they were meant to. Kiki’s mother tied a special flying star that would carry him and his sister across the sky. Lala’s father gave her a magical star wand to guide the twins on their journey. 

Check out more fun facts about our Friend of the Month:

Let’s meet Kiki and Lala’s plush toys:

Puff and Poff are Kiki and Lala’s favorites! The fairies made them out of the sky, rainbows, stars, and clouds especially for Kiki and Lala. The Little Twin Stars feel happy and safe with their plush toys.

Let’s meet the fairies:

Each fairy is unique and different in their own way, but they are all great friends of The Little Twin Stars.

Let’s meet the cloud animals:

Did you know Kiki and Lala can make animals out of clouds? They crafted each of their animal friends out of the clouds to accompany them!
