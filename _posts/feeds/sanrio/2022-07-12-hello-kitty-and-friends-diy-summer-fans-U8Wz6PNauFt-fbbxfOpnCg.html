Hello Kitty and Friends DIY Summer Fans

Hello Friends! Summer has arrived and the weather is heating up. Stay cool and look cool with these DIY fans you can make yourself!

Celebrate summertime with FREE Hello Kitty and Friends DIY Summer Fan downloads. Grab your materials and let's get crafting!

Hello Kitty and Friends DIY Summer Fan

Tools:

Select your fan template:

  • Keroppi download

  • Hello Kitty download

  • My Melody and Kuromi download

  • Scissors

  • Glue

  • String

  • Popsicle sticks

Optional:

  • Markers

Instructions

1. Print and cut out the downloadable and fold the paper accordion style.

2. Tie a string tightly in the middle of the accordion folded paper.

*Optional: color in your popsicle sticks as you like!

3. Glue one of the outside edges together, then glue two popsicle sticks to the other outside edge.

Voila! Your very own Sanrio-style fan to keep you cool in the summer heat. Enjoy!
