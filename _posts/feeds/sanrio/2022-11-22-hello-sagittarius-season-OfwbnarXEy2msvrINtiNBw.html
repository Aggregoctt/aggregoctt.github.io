Hello Sagittarius Season

It’s finally Sagittarius season! (November 22 - December 21)

Curious and energetic, Sagittarius are the travelers of the zodiac. They have open minds, full of philosophy and curiosity. Sagittarius is an extrovert, always optimistic, full of enthusiasm, and ready for changes.

Let’s learn more about our Sagittarius friends!

Sagittarius Strengths:

Generous, family-oriented, confident, great sense of humor

Sagittarius Likes:

Freedom, travel, being outdoors
